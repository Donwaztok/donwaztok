This policy describes how the application handles information when you use it. By using the app, you agree to the practices described here, to the extent applicable in your jurisdiction.

## 1. Data controller

The application is provided by **Donwaztok**. For privacy-related questions, contact: [igordonwaztok@gmail.com](mailto:igordonwaztok@gmail.com).

## 2. Data we do not collect directly

We do not require registration with name, email, or phone number to use the app. We do not operate an in-app “user account” system for sign-in purposes.

## 3. Data stored on your device

The app may store information **locally** on your phone or tablet, for example:

- interface preferences (e.g. light/dark theme);
- selected language;
- in-app purchase state (e.g. items purchased), using secure storage where applicable;
- data needed for promotional or first-launch features, as implemented in the app.

This data remains on the device unless a third-party integration (described below) processes it on their own systems.

## 4. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The app uses **Google Mobile Ads (AdMob)** to show ads. Google may collect and process identifiers and usage data for personalization, measurement, and fraud prevention, in accordance with Google’s policies.

On Android, the app may declare permission related to the **Advertising ID**, used in the ads ecosystem. You can manage or limit ad personalization in your system settings and Google account options.

Learn more: [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) and [AdMob information](https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543).

## 5. In-app purchases (Google Play / App Store)

In-app purchases are processed by **Google Play** and/or the **Apple App Store**. We do not receive or store your full card details; payment is handled by the store. We may receive from the store identifiers and transaction status needed to unlock purchased features.

Applicable policies: [Google](https://policies.google.com/privacy), [Apple](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).

## 6. App updates (Expo / EAS Update)

The app may receive feature or bugfix updates through **Expo Updates** (hosted by Expo). That may involve network requests to Expo’s servers to check for and download updates compatible with your app version. See [Expo’s privacy policy](https://expo.dev/privacy).

## 7. Children

The app is not directed at children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a child, contact us using the email in Section 1 so we can take appropriate steps.

## 8. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The date in the metadata at the top of this page indicates the last revision. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy, unless applicable law requires otherwise.

## 9. Your rights

Depending on your country or region (for example LGPD in Brazil or GDPR in the EU), you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data we process as a controller. To exercise those rights, use the contact in Section 1.
